Hi, I'm

Heather

I'm a Scottish astrophysicist investigating the link between planet-forming environments and their resulting planetary systems. Currently a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Exeter, my work focuses on disc evolution, giant planet formation, and the influence of host stars on protoplanetary systems. I completed my PhD at the University of Leeds modelling giant planet formation around intermediate-mass stars.
